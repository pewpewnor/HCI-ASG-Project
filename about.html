We Are The Yamada

Yamada has been continuously rated as the best motorcycle in the world. It achieves this title by winning in competitions such as the Pepega Olympics, Clueless Grand Finale, and the 2024 F2 Tokyo Race. Ever since it's founding in 2012 by Mr Felix Lengyel and the Global Twitch Gamba Solutions.

Why we do what we do.

We love what we do. We want to bring our customers the best experience in riding one of our motorcycles. To do this, we handcrafted motorcycle designs from scratch and tailer the seats. Our designs make it possible for every rider out there in the world to socialize and feel welcomed into the motorcycle rallies and clubs. Globally, we want our products to be beloved by our consumers.

What is our principle?

Our principle is quite simple, to deliver the best every single time. This meant that our 24/7 production must be at the highest quality possible. This way, we can guarantee that our product is safe to use since we prioritize customers' safety as well. No matter the cost, our customer service will always be here for you in time of need in every city in the world.
